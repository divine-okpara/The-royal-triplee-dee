Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How do I enroll my child at Royal Triple Dee College?

▾

Enrollment is simple. You can visit our school office in person, send us a WhatsApp message, or fill out the contact form above. Our admissions team will guide you through the documents needed, entrance assessments, and fee structure for your child's class level.

What are the school fees for Primary and Secondary divisions?

▾

School fees vary by class level and division. Please contact us directly via WhatsApp or phone for the current fee schedule, as we update it each academic session. Flexible payment plans are available for families.

How can I access my child's academic results online?

▾

You can check your child's results using our Result Checker tool on the website. You'll need the student ID and a PIN provided by the school at the end of each term. Contact the school office if you haven't received your PIN.

Does the school provide transportation for students?

▾

Yes, we have a school bus service covering selected routes within Ibeju Lekki and surrounding areas. Please contact our office for route availability, pickup schedules, and transport fees for the current term.

What extra-curricular activities do you offer?

▾

We offer football, basketball, athletics, debate club, drama, music, robotics/STEM club, press club, and environmental awareness club. Activities vary by division and are included in our school programme at no extra charge.
