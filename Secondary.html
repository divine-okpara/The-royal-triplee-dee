EDUCATION

The institution offers a blend of both Nigerian and British academic programmes designed to give students a well-rounded education that prepares them for the global stage. Our secondary programme focuses on critical thinking, moral excellence, and academic distinction.

EXAMINATIONS AVAILABLE

Royal Triple D College is fully accredited and recognized by the appropriate educational authorities. We prepare our students for the highest standards in national and international examinations.

  1. Junior Secondary Certificate Exams (BECE, Checkpoint)
  2. Senior Secondary Certificate Exams (WAEC, NECO)

OUR FACILITIES

The school is situated in a calm and conducive environment ideal for effective learning. We spare no expense ensuring our students have everything they need to excel.

  • A well-equipped multipurpose hall
  • Large, fully air-conditioned classrooms
  • Modern computer labs with high-speed internet access
  • Fully stocked science and biology laboratories
  • Sports courts and recreational facilities

TECHNOLOGY INTEGRATION

In today's digital age, technology plays a vital role in enhancing teaching and learning experiences. Our secondary school is equipped with the latest tools to keep students ahead of the curve.

We utilize modern classroom management tools such as NETSUPPORT to improve interaction between teachers and students, monitor engagement in real time, and ensure every learner is on track.

OUR CORE VALUES

With a strong foundation in "Faith in God", we are committed to nurturing disciplined, responsible, and compassionate students who will go on to lead their communities and nation with integrity and excellence.

We also provide dedicated support and careful attention to students with unique learning needs, ensuring that every child — regardless of their starting point — has the opportunity to grow, thrive, and succeed.
